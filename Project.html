TR-6 Multimodal Fruit Lifecycle Dataset

Published the TR-6 dataset on Figshare, comprising Thermal IR-Fusion images, sRGB images, and methane concentration readings to capture visual, thermal, and chemical changes in fruits from unripe to spoiled stages. The dataset includes 10,000+ images per split across six fruit types and is provided without preprocessing, making it suitable for machine learning, computer vision, and data-driven research applications.
